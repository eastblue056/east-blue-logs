Welcome to East Blue Logs

We're a passionate team of One Piece fans who've made it our mission to gather all available info about the series. This wiki is intended to provide information about One Piece. We try to keep the information as up to date as possible. This means that it is based on the status of the Japanese manga and newly published chapters.


Here, you'll find info on how Luffy met his first crewmates, the villains they faced, and the adventures that kicked off their quest for the One Piece. Whether you're a new fan or just want to relive the early days, East Blue Logs has got you covered.
